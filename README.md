## Style & Tone
- Institutional, high-trust, executive-level corporate tone
- Calm, restrained, conservative; no hype or sales language
- Typography-first layout with generous white space and strict hierarchy

## Visual Constraints
- No animations, no parallax, no gradients
- No stock photos or generic tech imagery
- Muted palette: deep navy, charcoal, slate gray, off-white background

## Typography
- Timeless, professional fonts (serif for headings, sans for body)
- Understated typographic scale and emphasis

## Structure (One-Page)
- Hero: title + short tagline + "Established 2020" (no CTAs)
- About: 1-2 short paragraphs on strategic digital infrastructure and licensing
- Value Pillars: 3-4 concise, executive-safe points
- Footer: LLC legal name, discreet email, privacy policy, copyright

## Technical
- Mobile-first, responsive, fast-loading
- Clean HTML/CSS, easy to update
- SEO-ready meta placeholders, HTTPS-ready
- Suitable for Vercel/Webflow/Carrd Pro deployment

## Refinements (Per Feedback)
- Slightly narrower hero headline width
- Tagline in a lighter gray
- Increased letter-spacing for "Established 2020"
- Value Pillars with subtle visual rule for hierarchy
- Shorter line length for About paragraphs
- Footer email more discreet; darkens on hover
